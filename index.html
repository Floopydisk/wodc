About WODC

The Women of Destiny Fellowship

  • Fellowship
  • Mission
  • Meetings
The Daystar Women of Destiny Fellowship is a vibrant and enriching fellowship of focused and remarkable women of God. Every woman in Daystar has a place in the Women of Destiny (WOD) fellowship.
To empower Daystar women to discover, develop, release and maximize our potentials in God. WOD has different groups and sub-units that reach out to women of different ages and status.
We meet on the first Saturday of every month at 10:00 a.m. We meet on-site at Ikosi Road, Oregun, and sometimes we meet online.
