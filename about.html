About

The Women of Destiny Fellowship

The Daystar Women of Destiny Fellowship is a vibrant and enriching fellowship of focused and remarkable women of God.

Our Mission

To empower Daystar women to discover, develop, release and maximize our potentials in God.

Our Meetings

First Saturday of every month at 10:00 a.m.

We meet on-site at Ikosi Road, Oregun, and sometimes have our meetings online.

Our Units

Fellowship Teams & Groups

Every woman in Daystar has a place in the Women of Destiny (WOD) fellowship. We have different groups and sub-units working together to reach out to women of all ages and status.

Golden Mothers

They cater to women of age 50 and above. If you are within this age group, you are automatically a member.

Princesses

This unit caters to the needs of single women. They meet to encourage and equip single ladies during the waiting period.

Wise Women

This unit is for widows who are of age, older women. We thank God who has been supporting our dear mothers over the years.

Dorcas Team

This unit caters to the needs of sisters who have been widowed at a young age. Those who are in this unit minister to them and serve in all capacity concerning them.

Joyful Mothers/Fathers Forum

This is the unit for waiting mothers and for pregnant women too. We have several members serving as spiritual midwives and attending to the needs of pregnant women.

Logistics Team

Consists of Ushering, Protocol, and Registration sub-units. They handle the operations of WOD monthly meetings and other special occasions.

Worship Team

They are the WOD Worship leaders who minister to the fellowship during meetings and events.

Membership Team

The team handles the welcome, follow-up, and integration of new members into the fellowship.

Visitation Team

They are in charge of members' visitation on any occasion or in any circumstances to provide care and support.

Publicity Team

They are in charge of publicity and promotion of all WOD events and meetings to reach more women.

Evangelism Team

The team goes out on evangelism outreach at chosen times and meets to pray for souls and women in general.

Welfare Team

They plan and coordinate WOD social occasions and cater to the needs of all WOD workers and guests during programs.

Prayer Team (Women on the Watch)

They meet to pray and intercede for all women and members of Daystar. They also manage our Prayer group tagged 'WOD War Room' which is open for all members.
